Biography

I completed my Master's Degree in the Department of Electrical Engineering at National Tsing Hua University in 2017. I was a member of Vision Science Lab advised by Prof. Min Sun.

My research topics are centered on computer vision, machine learning, especially in deep learning. My project focus on daliy life object recognition with wearable devices.
